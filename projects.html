Human Psychology

Understanding Human Behaviour Human behavior encompasses all physical, mental, and social actions and responses exhibited by individuals, ranging from simple reflexes to complex social interactions. It is influenced by a combination of genetic predispositions, brain function, personality traits, and environmental factors, including culture, social norms, and personal experiences. Psychological processes such as perception, learning, memory, and emotion play a central role in shaping behavior.
